Drums

The drum set is a collection of drums: snare, bass, toms and cymbals: hi-hat, crash, ride.
Drumming, whether playing accompaniment of voices and other instruments or doing a drum solo, consists of two main elements:
a groove which sets the basic time-feel and provides a rhythmic framework for the song, and drum fills which provide variety and add interest to the drum sound.


Guitar

The guitar is a fretted musical instrument that typically has six strings.
The sound of the acoustic guitar is projected acoustically, by means of a resonant chamber on the instrument, and the sound of the electric guitar is amplified by an electronic pickup and an amplifier.


Bass

The bass guitar is the lowest-pitched member of the guitar family.
It is a plucked string instrument similar in appearance and construction to an electric or an acoustic guitar, but with a longer neck and scale length, and typically four strings.
The bass is played primarily with the fingers or thumb, or sometimes with a pick.


Basic Guide

On this website, you can create musical tracks and songs. You may create each musical instrument track separately and then combine them.
In the 'create' page you'll find a matrix that represents a single bar divided to 16th notes, then chose the wanted bpm (slow: 40-70, medium: 80-110, fast: 120-180).
